How Our DAO Works

The CivicChain Africa DAO is a community-led governance platform. Members can submit proposals, vote on fund allocation, and monitor project milestones in real time. Every transaction is transparent and auditable on the blockchain.

Submit Proposals

Any member can submit a project or initiative for funding, detailing expected impact and milestones.

Participate in Voting

Members cast votes on proposals using a transparent, blockchain-verified process.

Milestone Funding

Funds are released in phases after milestone verification, ensuring accountability and measurable impact.

Impact Reports

All projects generate transparent, verifiable reports on-chain, accessible to every DAO member.

Community Governance

Members guide the organization’s priorities, ensuring community-led development across the continent.

Data Transparency

Blockchain analytics allow full auditability of fund flows and measurable impact metrics for every project.
